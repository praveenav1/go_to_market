# How to Submit a GTM Resource

## Overview

Want to share your GTM resource with the team? Follow these simple steps to submit your resource for approval. Once an admin reviews and approves it, your resource will be available to everyone in the GTM Repository!

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Navigate to the Submission Form
1. Open the GTM Repository application
2. Click the **"+ Add my GTM"** button in the top right
3. You'll be taken to the submission form

### Step 2: Fill in the Resource Header
- **What it is**: The title or name of your GTM resource
- **Examples**:
  - "Product Launch Strategy for Q3"
  - "Market Analysis Template"
  - "Sales Enablement Video Series"
- **Requirements**: Required field, keep it clear and concise

### Step 3: Write a Description
- **What it is**: A detailed explanation of what your resource contains
- **Include**:
  - What problem it solves
  - Key takeaways or benefits
  - How to use it
  - Who should access it
- **Length**: 2-3 sentences minimum (the more helpful, the better!)

### Step 4: Select or Create Tags
Tags help users find your resource when filtering. You have two options:

#### Option A: Use Existing Tags
- Click any of the pre-existing tags shown on the form
- These include: Launch, Strategy, Product, Market Research, Sales, Marketing, etc.

#### Option B: Create Custom Tags
- Type a new tag in the text box
- Press **Enter** or click **Add**
- Create tags that describe your resource

**Pro Tip**: Select **at least 2-3 tags** for better discoverability!

### Step 5: Upload Your Video
1. Click the **video upload area** (the dashed box)
2. Choose a video file from your computer
3. **Supported formats**:
   - MP4 (recommended - best compatibility)
   - WebM, AVI, MOV, MKV, FLV, M4V
4. **Size limit**: Maximum 500MB
5. The form will show which file you selected once it's picked

### Step 6: Submit for Approval
1. Review all your information
2. Click the **"Submit for Approval"** button
3. You'll see a confirmation message that your submission was received
4. An administrator will review your resource within 1-2 business days

## What Happens Next?

### Timeline
1. **Day 1**: Your submission received → appears in admin queue
2. **Day 1-2**: Admin reviews your resource
3. **Upon Approval**: Your resource automatically appears in the GTM Repository

### You'll Know It's Approved When
- ✅ Your resource appears on the main Repository page
- ✅ Other users can search and filter by your tags
- ✅ Your video is available for streaming

### If Changes Are Needed
- The admin may ask for modifications (usually via email/message)
- Make improvements and resubmit
- Resources can be resubmitted as many times as needed

## Best Practices for Approval

To help your submission get approved quickly, keep these tips in mind:

### Good Resource Headers
❌ "Video Demo" → ✅ "Product Launch Strategy - Q3 2026"  
❌ "Resource" → ✅ "Market Analysis and Segmentation Guide"  
❌ "Thing" → ✅ "Sales Enablement Toolkit with Templates"  

### Good Descriptions
✅ "Comprehensive guide covering market research methodology, customer segmentation strategies, and GTM campaign planning for enterprise accounts"  
✅ "This video walkthrough demonstrates our new product launch process, including timeline, stakeholder communication, and success metrics"  
✅ "Interactive sales enablement toolkit containing presentation templates, battle cards, and competitive positioning documents"  

### Good Tag Selection
- **Too few tags** (1): Resource harder to find
- **Too many tags** (6+): May be spamming categories
- **Just right** (2-4): Balanced for discovery
- **Use categories**: Product, Launch, Strategy, Marketing, Sales, etc.

### Video Quality Standards
- ✅ Clear audio that's easy to understand
- ✅ Good lighting and video quality (at least 720p)
- ✅ Relevant content that matches your description
- ✅ Professional presentation
- ❌ Blurry or low-resolution video
- ❌ Background noise or audio issues
- ❌ Content that doesn't match description

## FAQ

### How long does approval take?
Typically 1-2 business days. High-quality submissions may be approved within hours.

### Can I edit after submitting?
Currently, you'll need to submit a new version. The admin can help consolidate duplicates.

### What if my submission is rejected?
The admin will provide feedback explaining why. You can fix the issues and resubmit.

### Can I submit multiple resources?
Yes! Feel free to submit as many resources as you'd like.

### Is there a limit on video length?
No hard limit, but keep it reasonable. Most effective videos are under 10 minutes.

### Who can see my video after approval?
Everyone with access to the GTM Repository can search, find, and watch your resource.

## Troubleshooting

### Form Won't Submit
**Check**:
- ✓ Header is filled in
- ✓ Description is filled in
- ✓ At least one tag is selected
- ✓ Video file is selected
- ✓ Video file is under 500MB

**Still stuck?** Try refreshing the page and try again.

### Video Upload Failed
- Check file format (MP4 recommended)
- Check file size (must be under 500MB)
- Check your internet connection
- Try uploading a smaller test video first

### Can't Find the "Add my GTM" Button
- Refresh the page
- The button is in the top right corner of the page
- Look for white button with text "+ Add my GTM"
